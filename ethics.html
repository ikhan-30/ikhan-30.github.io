IT-Related Ethics

----------------------------

As technology advances, ethical considerations in IT become more important. Professionals in the field must prioritize fairness, privacy, and transparency.

Guidelines for Developers

  • Ensure secure and accessible software design.
  • Write clean, responsible code.
  • Respect user privacy.

Data Science Ethics

  • Avoid algorithmic bias.
  • Make data usage transparent.
  • Protect user identity and data.
